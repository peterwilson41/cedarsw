About Us What We Do Start a Conversation

The support behind a flourishing family.

However your wealth was built, through a company you grew, the sale of an operating business or a legacy passed down, Cedars Woods advises on the structures and strategies that suit it now and serve the generations to come. Our specialists plan sound arrangements across Singapore and Australia that keep a family's business interests and personal affairs working towards the same ends.

01

Wealth Management

We build and run portfolios around a family's goals and appetite for risk. Once we understand what a family is investing for, we set the strategy and place capital with the managers best suited to it. At Cedars Woods, portfolios are spread across asset classes and markets to stand up through changing conditions, with infrastructure and private market opportunities available where they earn their place.

02

Tax, Accounting & Advisory

A family with interests in Singapore and Australia carries obligations in both, and the two systems interact in ways that reward careful planning. We maintain the accounts and the reporting, and structure a family's affairs so they sit soundly across both jurisdictions. As laws shift and family's circumstances move, we keep the structure current, so there are no surprises.

03

Property Services

For families holding property as an investment or development, in one country or both, Cedars Woods manages the asset on your behalf. The work covers buyer's advocacy and acquisition, leasing and tenancy management, development and project management end to end, portfolio oversight across multiple holdings and sale or divestment when the time comes. You enjoy the property and the return while we handle the rest.

04

Business Succession

A founder reaches a point where the question becomes who comes next. Cedars Woods prepares the business and the family for a smooth handover. That work includes drafting family governance frameworks, establishing family councils, structuring the transfer of ownership and preparing the next generation for the responsibility ahead. Where a sale or partial divestment is the better path, we guide that too. Leadership passes into steady hands, protecting family harmony alongside your legacy.

05

Lifestyle Asset Management

A family's assets are also there to be enjoyed. A home abroad, a yacht, a jet, a collection, we look after these assets on the family's behalf, drawing on a network of brokers and specialists to keep everything ready for use. Whether a family owns the asset or wants it for a season, the arrangement falls to us, and the enjoyment stays with them.

06

Philanthropy

Giving is often where a family expresses what it cares about most. Some families prefer to give directly to causes and organisations they know, and we facilitate that support. Others want a vehicle of their own, whether a charitable trust, a private foundation structure or an endowment built to fund causes over the long term, and we establish whichever suits. From there, we manage the running of it, so the giving keeps its shape over the years. Several of our families support the social causes below across Singapore and Australia.
